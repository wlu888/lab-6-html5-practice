Where should people visit in Jeonju? What can I do there?

Jeonju Heritage Tour offers three activities for guided visits that allow visitors to enjoy history and culture of South Korea, while avoiding the hustle and bustle of Seoul.

Jeonju Hanok Village

Explore the traditional village renowned for its well-preserved hanok houses, which are traditional Korean houses with a unique architectural style.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Activities: Hanok stay, rent and wear hanboks (traditional Korean clothing)
  • Price: 7000 won/hr

Bibimbap Festival

Join us in the heart of Jeonju for a culinary celebration like no other! Indulge in the tantalizing flavors and vibrant colors of bibimbap, Korea's iconic dish perfected over generations.

  • Duration: 1-2 day(s)
  • Activities: Cooking competition, games, workshops, live music
  • Price: Free!

History of Jeonju

Jeonju, a city possessing one-thousand years of history Jeonju, a city where the old meets the new. It is a city with strong ties to kings and democratic movements. Some of the greatest achievements in history took place here in Jeonju.
